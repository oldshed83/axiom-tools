FAQ

Common questions.

Straight answers, nothing hidden.

Is Nyrus safe to use?
Yeah. It's a bookmarklet — just a tiny bit of JS that runs in your tab on the Axiom page. No servers, no wallet access, nothing gets collected.
Does it touch my wallet?
No. Nyrus only interacts with the Axiom page interface to update settings. It has zero access to your private keys, seed phrase, or any connected wallet.
Which browsers work?
Chrome, Brave, and Arc work great. Edge should also work. Firefox is mostly fine but less tested — if you run into something, let us know.
Do I have to redo this every session?
Yep, one click per session. The bookmark stays in your bar permanently — just click it when you open Axiom and your settings are loaded. They stick until you close the tab or refresh.
Is this affiliated with Axiom?
Nope — Nyrus is a fully independent community project, not affiliated with or endorsed by Axiom in any way. It just works on top of their existing interface.
Is it free?
Completely. No premium tier, no token, no subscription. Free now, free forever.
